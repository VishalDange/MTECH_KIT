WELCOME TO KIT College of Engineering, Kolhapur

Established in 1983 by a group of visionary industrialists, professionals and educationalists, KIT College of Engineering has been awarded “A Grade Status with CGPA 3.12” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bengaluru. Situated in Kolhapur, KIT College of Engineering is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).The institute has also received permanent affiliation by the Shivaji University Kolhapur and has received Autonomous status. The National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi, which is the Government body for the accreditation of programs in institutes, has granted accreditation to the Institute for six departments (Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Production Engineering). With more than 3,000 enrolled students and around 180 registered faculty members, the institute offers 22 various undergraduate and postgraduate Engineering courses across various disciplines.
